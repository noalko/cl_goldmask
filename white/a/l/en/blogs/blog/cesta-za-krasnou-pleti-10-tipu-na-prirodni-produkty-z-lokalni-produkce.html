The Journey to Beautiful Skin: 10 Tips for Natural Products from Local Production

Ester Sidibe
07 February, 2020

  • #Sustainability

Cosmetic products which are both natural and local? We call this a double win! Our skin suffers in winter and therefore we need to give it the best possible care. Where to find an effective deodorant and why can't we go without flower water? We bring you a selection of 10 natural products that will make your wait for spring much more pleasant.

 

Mylo Serum Flora

Dry and hypersensitive skin is a problem for many of us from time to time, especially in winter. The Flora Serum is created specifically for the sensitive skin of the face. Its charm lies in the combination of easily absorbable oils that gently get the necessary vitamins, hydration and provide protection from UV radiation. Products of the Slovak brand Mylo are availablel in NILADaily in Prague's Vinohrady.

 

Havlík's Natural Miracle

As the name suggests, this ointment is truly miraculous. I prefer to use it as a nourishing mask, applying it on my skin in the evening after taking my make-up and leaving it until morning. Nourishing ointment is drunk into the skin during the night and it is beautifully soft and nourished after waking up.

 

Biorythme Deodorant Rose Garden

Finding a natural deodorant that works and at the same time smells pleasant is not easy. Luckily, Biorythme deodorants do both. In addition, you do not have to buy a new pack after it has been used up. The fragrance of the Rose Garden is pleasantly intense, but at the same time soft enough not to take on the scent of your perfume. But the brand also offers a completely fragrance-free variant, so that everyone can find their own.

 

Malinna Elixir Mint

Elixir Mint from the Slovak family brand Malinna begins the journey for beautiful skin and healthy body from the inside. The unique composition of herbs, essential and organic oils and sea salt contributes to improving immunity and overall regeneration of the organism. It is suitable as a morning drink additive or as an alternative to toothpaste.

 

Nobilis Tillia Hydrophilic Facial Oils

Nobillis Tillia products are my “go to”, because they do exactly what I expect from multifunctional products. You can use skin oils as the first step of evening skin cleansing, like massage oil or even as a hair nutrition. Gently gets rid of all the remnants of makeup, nourishes the skin and also smells beautiful. Thanks to gentle composition it does not disturb the skin barrier.

 

Flower Water Nobilis Tilia

Flower has been one of my stars on the cosmetic shelf lately. My favorite is the rose water Nobilis Tilia. It is an ideal supplement to the use of facial oils, as they work much better on moistened skin. Flowered water does not dry compared to tap water, but on the contrary, beautifully prepares the skin for oil.

 

Cannor Miracle Regenerative Oil

Another helper for dry, sensitive and stressed skin. Because one is never enough! In addition, CBD has a high content of omega 6 fatty acids, vitamins, minerals and antioxidants.

 

Guasha Rose Quartz Beloved Shop 

Guasha ideally adapts to your face shape, freeing skin from toxic substances, and also has beneficial effects on your mental health. A few drops of serum or oil and the encouraging morning ritual is born!

 

Inlight Bio Super-Food Mask

When it comes to face masks, this is number one in the local cosmetics market. 100% natural product, additionally made from ingredients from organic farming. This “miracle in the box” instantly revives pale, greyish and tired skin. You can get it at the Bioorganica shop.

 

Ecce Vita

Ecce Vita knows that beauty comes from within. In beautiful shop near Anděl you will find a wealth of vitamins and nutritional supplements, which naturally help balance the body. In addition, Ecce Vita always prepares products tailored to your current needs.
